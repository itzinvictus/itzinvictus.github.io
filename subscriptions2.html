Frequently Asked Questions

What is Season 0 and why is Contender tier featured? +
Season 0 is our startup season where Contender tier organizations compete for monthly prize pools. The org with the highest record at month's end wins $200. After Season 0, this tier will be removed and Challenger becomes the new entry level.
How does the monthly prize pool work for Contender tier? +
With 20+ organizations in Contender tier at $10/month each, we guarantee a $200 monthly prize pool. The organization with the best win-loss record at the end of each month receives the full prize.
What's the difference between Rose Bowl and Super Bowl divisions? +
Rose Bowl Division is for mid-tier competitive teams (Challenger tier), while Super Bowl Division is our premier circuit for elite organizations. Legacy tier gets guaranteed Super Bowl placement, while Elite tier can earn promotion through cross-division matches.
Can I upgrade my organization's tier anytime? +
Yes! You can upgrade at any time and gain immediate access to higher tier benefits. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. Legacy tier is a one-time payment with permanent benefits.
